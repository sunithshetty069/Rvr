RVR Super Speciality Centre was built to change that. Permanently. With the most advanced medical equipment in the region, an extraordinary team of specialists, and an AI-powered system that rivals any hospital in the world.

This is not just a hospital. This is the beginning of something much larger — a healthcare ecosystem that will grow across India and reach the world.

The most advanced systems
ever seen in Mangalore

01
AI Clinical Documentation
Sully.ai
The doctor speaks, the AI writes. Zero typing. Clinically accurate notes in real time. Prescriptions auto-generated and pushed to pharmacy the moment the doctor approves.
02
WhatsApp-First Patient Experience
Wati
Every patient interaction automated — confirmations, lab reports, medicine reminders, follow-ups — delivered on WhatsApp 24/7. Zero manual effort from staff.
03
International Hospital Management System
HMS
International-grade HMS used across 23+ countries. Real-time director dashboards. Role-based access. Complete EMR, pharmacy, lab and billing in one system.
04
Most Advanced Equipment in Coastal Karnataka
Industry First
State-of-the-art neurology, cardiology and imaging technology no other hospital in the region possesses. Patients no longer need to travel to Bangalore or Chennai.

Instant Doctor Payouts
Razorpay Route
Doctors see earnings in real time and transfer to their bank accounts instantly. Transparent, automated. No delays, no manual reconciliation.
